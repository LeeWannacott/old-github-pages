1. Research Neuroplasticity

Discuss how understanding the principles of neuroplasticity benefits people:

The principles of neuroplasticity help people in the sense that it allows individuals to make new pathways in the brain which may help individuals to learn new skills quicker than if the brain was at fixed state and couldn't make new pathways/connections.

Discuss how you might engage with the principles of neuroplasticity for your own benefit

By trying out new activities that the brain isn't used to performing; the brain will make new pathways ,or strengthen pathways that already exist, but are underutilized. For example learning an instrument, doing brainteasers, puzzles or Sudoku if you never do it before, or done the problem before.

What are some of the ways to increase your neuroplasticity?

Learning a new language (might apply to programming languages as well?). Travelling to new environments (new countries?). Make art, music, creative endeavours (programming has a creative aspect; creativity within the bounds of logic).
